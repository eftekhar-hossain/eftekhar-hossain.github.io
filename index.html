Eftekhar Hossain

Lecturer.

I'm a Lecturer at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), Bangladesh. My primary research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Specifically, I try to develop resources as well as computational models for Multilingual NLP. Currently, my research focuses on Multimodality in machine learning. I am trying to formulate ways so that we can effectively integrate the information of multiple modalities (i.e., image, text) and jointly evaluate them to solve complex language and vision problems. Besides, I am enthusiastic about exploring how we can distill the knowledge of high resource languages to develop or improve the efficiency of computational tools for resource constraint languages. I am a research member of CUET NLP Lab and am fortunate to work with a sage like Omar Sharif.

Previously, I received my B.Sc. (2014-2018) in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) from CUET. Afterward, I worked as a Lecturer at Premier University, Bangladesh. I have been passionate about research since my undergraduate. During that time, I did several small projects in different areas including Embedded system design , Signal and image processing, and web development which helped me to develop skills in Microcontroller, C, Python, Php, and Latex.
